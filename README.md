## Project Overview
The **Adventure Works Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an **Excel-based business analytics project** designed to analyze **sales performance, profitability, and customer transaction patterns**.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into **actionable business insights** using Excel formulas, pivot tables, charts, and slicers. It provides both high-level and detailed views of revenue, profit, product performance, and time-based trends.

This is a **descriptive analytics** project focused on understanding historical business performance.

---

## Business Problem
Business stakeholders often struggle to:
- Track revenue and profit performance efficiently  
- Identify top-performing products  
- Understand sales trends over time  
- Compare weekday vs weekend sales behavior  
- Analyze performance across regions and time periods  

Raw Excel data alone does not make these insights immediately visible.

This project solves that problem by presenting key metrics in a **single interactive Excel dashboard**.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales, profit, and cost performance  
- Identify top-selling and most profitable products  
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends  
- Compare weekday vs weekend sales contribution  
- Enable interactive filtering by time and country

## Dashboard Analysis
The dashboard provides insights into:

- **Key KPIs**
  - Total Quantity Sold  
  - Total Revenue  
  - Total Profit  
  - Profit Margin  

- **Sales Trends**
  - Monthly sales trend line  
  - Year-wise performance comparison  

- **Profit Analysis**
  - Profit by weekday  
  - Identification of highest and lowest profit days  

- **Product Performance**
  - Top 5 products by revenue  
  - Product-wise contribution to total sales  

- **Customer Behavior**
  - Weekday vs weekend sales distribution  

- **Filters**
  - Month slicer  
  - Country slicer  

These elements allow users to interactively explore business performance.

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue and profit show noticeable variation across months  
- Certain products contribute disproportionately to total revenue  
- Weekday sales account for a significantly larger share than weekend sales  
- Profitability varies by day of the week  
- Country-level filtering reveals regional performance differences
